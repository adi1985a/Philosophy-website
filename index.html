Latest Posts

Below you'll find the three most recently published articles - making it easy to check what's new on the blog. Posts usually appear on Tuesdays and Fridays, and I also announce updates on my Twitter.

Anthony de Mello: Seeking Acceptance

Someone once beautifully put it: life is what happens to us while we are busy making plans. We are busy impressing everyone around us, making sure to be well received. Let's talk about the addiction to seeking acceptance!

Fryderyk Nietzsche: Slaying the Dragon

The camel is an animal on which people place many burdens, including the weight of lacking autonomy. The camel (as camels do) goes to the desert with all this weight. What is the desert? It's a place where you are alone, where you don't hear all those "shoulds".

Alan Watts: The Need for Control

I wonder what you would do if you had the power to dream any dream you wanted at night. And of course, you would have the power to influence your perception of time in the dream. You could live, for example, 75 years of subjective time within an eight-hour sleep.
