About the Author

Get to know the person behind Philosophia Blog

My Journey

As a lifelong student of philosophy, my journey began in college where I discovered Eastern philosophy through Alan Watts' lectures.

This led me to explore Western philosophical traditions, particularly the works of Nietzsche and Kant. Through years of study and contemplation, I've developed a unique perspective that bridges Eastern and Western thought.

I believe that philosophy should be accessible to everyone, not just academics. That's why I started this blog - to share these transformative ideas in a way that connects with modern readers.

My Library

Over the years, I've built a collection of philosophical works that have shaped my thinking.

My library represents a journey through different schools of thought, from ancient wisdom to contemporary philosophy. Each book has contributed to my understanding and perspective.

Through this blog, I share insights from these works, making complex philosophical ideas more digestible and relevant to everyday life.
